Methodology. Recency window applies to the cohort-axis date selected above; rows whose cohort date falls outside the window are excluded entirely from the analytics on this tab. A row is "closed" when its application_status_code is 150 (Patented) or 161 (Abandoned); rows still pending are excluded from the Traditional and First-Action denominators but counted in Pendency.
